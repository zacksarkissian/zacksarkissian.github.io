Bio

At a young age, I didn’t know music would later become my passion. My parents bought me an acoustic guitar and signed me up for guitar lessons, but beyond having an affinity with the instrument, I wasn’t motivated to learn how to sight read and practice at home. Little did I know that 8 years later, in high school, the sound of an electric guitar would hit me right to my soul. I was hooked. Call it teenage angst or just a sound to express how I felt inside, something inside me woke up that day. It felt natural to express myself with a guitar in hand. From angry to happy, I could let it all out on chord at a time. The first time around, I was given a guitar. Now, I was being called to it!

When I'm not on my six string, you can find me in the studio producing new music or behind a soundboard mixing events such as festivals, corporate or live shows. Musician's Institute in LA is where I got to learn and sharpen my skills as a sound engineer.

Whether I'm on a stage playing or in front of the stage mixing the show, there is nothing like the thrill and the raw energy a live performance can get out of you. It's like trying to catch lightining in a bottle, you never know what to expect. Let the music do the talking!
